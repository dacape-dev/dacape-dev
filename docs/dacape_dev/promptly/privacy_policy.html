Privacy Policy

dacape.dev built the Promptly app as a free application supported by advertising. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using the Promptly application.

By using Promptly, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Because Promptly uses advertising (AdMob), certain data may be collected by third-party providers to display personalized or non-personalized ads.

Important Notice – Not Directed to Children

Promptly is not intended for children under the age of 16. The app includes advertising and analytics services that may collect data not suitable for a child-directed service.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used Promptly, please uninstall the app immediately.

Information Collection and Use

Promptly operates entirely on the user's device. The prompt library, variables, favorites, and usage history are stored locally and are never uploaded to any external server.

No prompts, personal content, or user-generated data are transmitted to dacape.dev or any third party.

Promptly does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names or email addresses.

Any analytics or crash data collected is handled exclusively by third-party services according to their respective privacy policies.

These third-party services may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage events and interactions
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

Third-Party Services

Promptly uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Log Data

In case of an error, Promptly collects technical information via Firebase Crashlytics. This may include device model, operating system version, app state at the time of the crash, and the date and time of the event.

No prompts, favorites, or personal user content are included in crash reports.

Cookies

Promptly does not use cookies directly. However, advertising partners such as Google AdMob or Google services may use cookies or similar technologies.

Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies to facilitate the Service, provide advertising, perform analytics, and monitor app performance.

Security

All data transmitted by third-party services used in Promptly is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.

Links to Other Sites

Promptly may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
