By using BreakFlow, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Because BreakFlow uses advertising (AdMob), certain data may be collected by third-party providers to display personalized or non-personalized ads.

Important Notice – Not Directed to Children

BreakFlow is not intended for children under the age of 16. The app includes advertising and analytics services that may collect data not suitable for a child-directed service.

Information Collection and Use

BreakFlow does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names, email addresses, or photos.

Any analytics or crash data collected is handled exclusively by third-party services according to their respective privacy policies.

These third-party services may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage events and interactions
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Log Data

In case of an error, BreakFlow collects technical information via Firebase Crashlytics, such as device model, operating system version, app state at the time of the crash, and the date and time of the event.

No personal content or user-generated data is included in crash reports.

Cookies

BreakFlow does not use cookies directly. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies.

Service Providers

Third-party companies may be used to provide advertising, analytics, and app performance monitoring.

Security

All data transmitted by third-party services used in BreakFlow is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

Links to Other Sites

BreakFlow may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.
