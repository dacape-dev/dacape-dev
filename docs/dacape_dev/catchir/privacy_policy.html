Privacy Policy

Catchir is developed by dacape.dev. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the app.

Information Collection and Use

Catchir does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personally identifiable information.

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, including:

  • Google AdMob (advertising services)
  • Firebase Analytics (usage statistics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reports and diagnostics)

These services may collect device information such as device type, operating system version, advertising identifiers, usage data, IP address, and crash logs. This data is used to provide ads, analyze usage, and improve the stability and performance of the app.

Third-Party Service Providers

Catchir uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Terms

We do not sell personal data. Data collected by third-party services is handled according to their respective privacy policies.

Advertising

Catchir displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and cookies to show relevant ads based on user activity. Users can reset or limit ad tracking through their device settings.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Catchir is not intended for children under 16 years of age, due to advertising and third-party data collection (AdMob).
