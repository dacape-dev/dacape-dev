Information Collection and Use

Snoutly performs dog breed identification entirely on the user's device using offline machine learning models.

No photos are uploaded, stored, or processed on external servers. All image analysis happens locally on your device.

Snoutly does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names or email addresses.

Any analytics or crash data collected is handled exclusively by third-party services according to their respective privacy policies.

These third-party services may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage events and interactions
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

Third-Party Services

Snoutly uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Log Data

No images, photos, or personal user-generated content are included in crash reports.
