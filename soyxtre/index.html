Story of Creation

This piece was inspired by the quiet solitude of midnight gardens. Every tuft was punched by hand over the course of 45 hours, layering distinct shades of midnight blue and charcoal to create depth and movement. The organic shapes represent the untamed growth of nature in darkness, crafted to bring a grounding, artisanal presence to your space.
